Our Mission

Wistriva empowers individuals navigating career transitions through one-on-one mentorship, peer support, and access to real-world insights. Whether you’re switching domains or restarting your journey, we help you grow with clarity and confidence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where access to mentorship is universal, career change is courageous, and every professional feels supported—not alone—when pursuing change.

Core Values

  • Empathy: We listen deeply and lead with understanding.
  • Authenticity: Real mentors, real stories, real growth.
  • Community: You’re never alone in your transition—Wistriva is your tribe.
  • Impact: Our goal is not just direction, but transformation.

Our Story

Wistriva began as a simple idea—career change should feel empowering, not isolating. Founded by industry switchers who faced the chaos of change firsthand, we built Wistriva to create what we wish we had: a network of guides, a hub of advice, and a community that gets it.
