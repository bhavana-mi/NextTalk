Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wistriva?
Wistriva is a mentorship platform connecting professionals across industries to share knowledge, support career transitions, and build networks.
How do I find a mentor?
You can browse mentors on the Mentors page and connect directly for personalized guidance based on your goals and career path.
Is there a fee to use Wistriva?
Basic networking and community features are free. Advanced 1:1 mentorship sessions may require a nominal fee depending on the mentor's terms.
How do I become a mentor?
Visit the Mentors page and apply to join our mentor community. We review all applications to ensure quality guidance.
How is my privacy protected?
We take privacy seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for details on data protection and your rights.
